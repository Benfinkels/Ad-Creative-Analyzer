# YouTube Ad ABCD Analyzer - User Guide

## Introduction

The YouTube Ad ABCD Analyzer is a tool designed to help you analyze your video advertisements based on Google's ABCD creative framework. By uploading your ad, you can get an expert analysis that provides insights and recommendations for improving its effectiveness.

## Getting Started

To get started, simply visit the application's URL: [https://ad-analyzer-sandbox-735825.ue.r.appspot.com](https://ad-analyzer-sandbox-735825.ue.r.appspot.com)

## How to Use

1.  **Upload Your Video:** Click the "Choose File" button to select the video ad you want to analyze from your local computer.
2.  **Select Marketing Objective:** Choose the primary marketing objective for your ad from the dropdown menu. This will help tailor the analysis to your specific goals.
3.  **Analyze:** Click the "Analyze" button to begin the analysis. This process may take a minute, so please be patient.
4.  **View Report:** Once the analysis is complete, a detailed report will be displayed on the screen. The report will provide a scorecard with ratings for different aspects of your ad, as well as actionable recommendations for improvement.
5.  **Export to Google Slides:** You can export the report to a Google Slides presentation by clicking the "Export to Slides" button. You will be prompted to sign in with your Google account and grant the necessary permissions.

## Troubleshooting

*   **Blank Page:** If you see a blank page, please try refreshing the page. If the issue persists, contact the support team.
*   **Export to Slides Error:** If you encounter an error while exporting to Google Slides, make sure you have granted the necessary permissions. If the issue persists, please contact support.
